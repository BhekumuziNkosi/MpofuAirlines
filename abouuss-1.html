Our Story


MpofuAirlines is a world-class international airline proudly based in South Africa. Founded and led by Bhekumuzi Nkosisiza Mpofu, the visionary CEO and owner, we are committed to delivering top-tier local and international air travel experiences.


Our fleet and service are built around comfort, safety, and luxury. From sunny South Africa to global destinations like Dubai, USA, Chicago, France, South Africa, Zimbabwe, Australia, and UK we got your travel dreams covered!
